What is Marketing and its importance?

Marketing is a program, a study and a process.
  • A Program to create awareness among potential clients.
  • A Study to understand different markets/marketable objects, needs/wants of customers/consumers. Often referred as Research and Analysis.
  • A Process to convince customers/consumers intersted in your markets/marketable objects.
The share of these factors (Program, Study, and Process) changes according to market and era.
